Car Glow Hockey

Car Glow Hockey is a collaboration project with Stan de Haas and Yaël Boersma. It is game that combines the physical world with the virual world.
Car Glow Hockey has a virtual play field that is projected on the ground. The goal is to score more points than the opponent. This is done by moving the virtual ball with the cars.
